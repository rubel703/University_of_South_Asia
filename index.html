Our Campus

The University of South Asia has two campuses, the permanent campus of Gabtali Amin Bazar in Dhaka. The 2nd City Campus is located in Banani, the main place of Dhaka. Educational activities have been started on the campus approved by the University Grants Commission in 2003.

Our Facilities

The University of South Asia has a Library, Gymnasium, Playground, medium of Debate competition, Music and Multi-Cultural system.

Class Library

Our library consists of three thousand rich books. There are various books related to engineering. There are also numerous books on society, science, environment. About 200 students will be able to study together here.

Debate Programm

University of South Asia is one of the debating programs. We have been champions several times in the debate program and were in a losing position last year. We are currently in the semifinal position.

Others Activities

The University of South Asia hosts a variety of social and cultural events each year. Computer programming competitions are held here every year. And various events including Bengali New Year, annual educational tour.
